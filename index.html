Introduction

I was one of the best jewel-thieves in the world - until I stole the fabled Awazaki Diamond, a stone with a bizarre curse upon it. Now, I have transformed in an unexpected way - I have strange new powers, but can I find a way of breaking the curse? I have the ability to transform my skin into a layer of diamond, making me almost indestructible! Now, I protect Prarietown from terrorists, while also battling the evil plans of Cold Henry!
